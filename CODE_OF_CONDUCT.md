# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a respectful, professional, and collaborative experience for everyone.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, inclusive, and constructive environment.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- showing respect for differing viewpoints and experiences
- giving and receiving constructive feedback gracefully
- focusing on what is best for the project and community
- demonstrating professionalism in technical discussion
- being considerate in written and verbal communication

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- harassment, discrimination, or abusive language
- personal attacks or trolling
- publishing others’ private information without permission
- intentionally disruptive behavior
- unprofessional or hostile conduct in issues, discussions, or pull requests

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards of acceptable behavior and may take appropriate and fair corrective action in response to unacceptable behavior.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces, including issues, pull requests, discussions, documentation reviews, and other project-related interactions.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it privately to the repository owner or maintainers through an appropriate private communication channel.

## Attribution

This Code of Conduct is adapted in spirit from common open-source community standards, including the Contributor Covenant.
